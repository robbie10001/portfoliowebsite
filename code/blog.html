30th of August 2019, It Takes A Community

Starting a coding bootcamp is not something to take up lightly. It requires looks days and extra study on the weekend. Luckily, Harry is really good at Ruby so he can help me when I struggle. Hopefully, I can repay the favour later in the course!

4th of September 2019, CoderAcademy VS Universities.

One of the best things about the boot camp, is the ability to help other students, this allows you to cement your own subject knowledge and make it feel like we are one big community. This is so different from the overly competitive, individualistic style of education offered at universities in Australia. I have really enjoyed this change of culture and hopefully, my next job will be comprised of an equally supportive team.
