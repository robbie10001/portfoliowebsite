My Educational and Professional History

I was educated from pre-school to year 12 at the International Grammar School. I enjoyed my time at school although I found it difficult to always apply myself completely to my studies. Following school, I decided to pursue a Bachelor of Arts degree at Macquarie University. I enjoyed my time and majored in Philosophy, Politics and International Relations. After my time at university, I ended up in television/film production. For the past few years, I have worked in the Art Department on a variety of different shows. However, I felt like it was time for a change. That’s why I enrolled in the CoderAcademy Fast Track Program. While it’s very challenging I’m enjoying it heaps!

Interests and Hobbies

Since my youth, I have enjoyed sports. I never really minded which ones. I spent a lot of time playing both cricket and basketball. As I grow older, I developed an interest in coin collecting, which is relatively unique for someone my age. I guess it was a reflection of a great deal of free time! I love to spend time with my family and friends and consider these relationships my most valued possession.
